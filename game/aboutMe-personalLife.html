Hobbies

Outside of my academic life, I like to do many things during my free time. When I am on my pc, I like to play games with my boyfriend and friends. When I'm not on my pc, I like to take a break by playing and hanging out with my dogs. One little short corgi and beagle mix named Kobe and the other is a border collie mutt named Zico. They are so adorable and cute but they can be a little chaotic sometimes. I also like to hang out with my sister and mom whenever they are free. Some of my hobbies are crocheting, painting, and drawing. I usually like to do this while listening to music. Some artists I like listening to is Doja Cat, Juice WRLD, 80purppp, and many more. In addition, I also like watching YouTube or watching anime. Some animes I've finished recently and enjoyed is My Hero Academia, Violet Evergarden, and Toilet-bound Hanako-kun. Although I don’t do this as often as I used to, I always try to do it from time to time.

Art Projects

Ever since I was little, I’ve always liked doing art like painting and drawing. One of my most recent projects I’ve finished is a white charcoal drawing of a man on a black piece of paper. I did this project for my Art 1 class and I consider it to be one of my most favorite drawings. Another piece I’ve finished recently is a black and white charcoal drawing done on a gray piece of paper, consisting of 3 different sized pots and bowls. This drawing was one of the simpler drawings I did but I really like the concept of drawing and shading both the shadows and the highlights of the pots. In addition, some paintings I’ve finished are this Bob Ross landscape painting I did in my Advanced Art class in 8th grade of a forest and a waterfall flowing through it. Another painting is a Bob Ross landscape painting I finished during the summer in 8th grade of a sunset on a beach. I had done this painting before but I didn’t really like the result of the painting the first time so I redid it and I like the outcome much better the second time. Lastly, a more hands on project I did was a slab pot I did with clay inspired by the character BMO from Adventure Time. To see more details about these projects and the other projects, come see my art portfolio.

Gaming

Whenever I have free time, I will hop on my pc to play games with my boyfriend and friends. Some games I play are Valorant, which is a 5v5 character-based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agents with different abilities. Another game I like to play is Fortnite, a battle royale which is like an arena where you have to be the last one standing. However, unlike other battle royales, you can build which makes the game a lot harder and more interesting. Minecraft, a game with many different adventure modes where you build, mine, fight mobs, and explore. From time to time, I will go to Steam and see what games look interesting. Some games I’ve downloaded from Steam are It Takes Two, a game where you and a friend embark on the craziest journey to work together across a huge variety of challenges. Muck, a survival game where you collect resources, find items, and build a base to survive for as long as possible. There are a lot more but those are the five games I played and enjoyed the most.

5-Year Plan

My plan for the next five years is to graduate high school and get my diploma after the first year. During my senior year, I plan on getting a permit then my license which will allow me to drive to and from school, home, and a part time job. I’m not sure where I am going to be working but I really want a retail job at a mall or something because I don’t really want to work at a fast food place but no other place accepts me. I probably will have to. After graduating, I will move to Los Angeles and go to community college with my boyfriend for the next two years. I’m not sure about my living space because we could move out together or live with his parents. After finishing community college, I am going to transfer over to a university college and currently I’m not sure what university it is going to be. While I’m attending college, I am going to also want to have a part time job and hopefully a license by then. After finishing college, I am going to look for a job in the computer industry. Hopefully either as a web designer or a game designer.
